// Project Brief

A love letter
with a t-pose

Petty Theft Amazing VI: Floridada is a hand built, to scale, recreation of an upcoming title's map in Unity, based upon the trailers, fan speculation, and primarily the mapping project.

PTA6 is aimed as a tribute to this upcoming title, and is made by fans for fans. The project is aiming to have a fully fledged campaign built on the foundation of our highly-inspired map.

PTA6 is a completely non-profit, non-revenue-in-any-way fan work - this means no donations, or paid versions, just simply the project of a couple of fans having fun and making something to tide the wait until this upcoming title.

FAQ

Is this really free?

Yes. There is no money anywhere in this project. We do not take donations, sell anything, or run ads. If someone is charging you for it, they're the actual petty thief.

Is this the real title I think it is?

No. It is a fan-made parody assembled from public community information. The real game you're thinking of is made by a very popular, well-funded studio. We are made by people in a group chat.

Why does everyone t-pose?

Animation is hard and free is free. Consider the t-pose a deliberate artistic statement about confidence.
